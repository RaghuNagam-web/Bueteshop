FAQs

Is BueteShop free to use?

Yes! Browsing coupons, deals, and offers is completely free for all users. Merchants can also register and upload offers — basic listings are free, and we offer optional paid slots for better visibility.

How can I register on BueteShop?

  • Click on “Register” on the top right corner.
  • Select User if you want to browse and save offers.
  • Select Merchant if you want to promote your offers.
  • Fill in the required details and verify your email — that’s it!

I’m a Merchant. How can I upload my offer or ad post?

After registering and logging in:

  • Go to your Merchant Dashboard.
  • Click Upload Offer.
  • Fill in the offer details, upload images, and choose your display slot.
  • Submit for Admin approval — once approved, it will go live.

What are Ad Slots, and how do they work?

Ad Slots are special spots on the homepage or featured sections where your offers get extra visibility. Merchants can:

  • Choose free randomized slots
  • Or purchase premium slots for guaranteed placement and positioning.

How long will my offer be visible?

Each offer stays active until its expiry date, which you set while uploading. Expired offers are automatically removed from public display.

Can I edit or delete my uploaded offer?

Yes. Just log into your Merchant Dashboard:

  • Go to My Offers
  • Click Edit or Delete next to the offer you want to manage.

How are offers approved?

All offers go through a quick admin review process to ensure:

  • Content is relevant and non-offensive
  • No fake or misleading promotions
  • Once approved, they go live almost instantly.

Do you support regional languages for offers?

Yes, merchants can upload content in English, Hindi, or Telugu. Our platform is built for a multilingual audience.

How can I contact BueteShop support?

You can reach us through:

  • Contact Us page on the website
  • Or email us at support@bakpoket.com

Is my personal data safe on BueteShop?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and privacy protocols to ensure your data is safe and never sold to third parties.

Do I need to install any app?

Nope! BueteShop is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop browsers. Just visit bueteshop.com — no app needed.
