Careers at BueteShop

Support Executive

1. Customer Interaction

  • Respond to customer queries via phone, email, chat, or ticketing systems.
  • Provide accurate information and troubleshooting steps for issues reported.
  • Maintain a positive and empathetic attitude toward customers.

2. Feedback & Reporting

  • Record customer feedback and pain points.
  • Create regular reports on recurring issues or customer satisfaction.
  • Suggest improvements to workflows or FAQs.

3. Collaboration

  • Coordinate with internal departments (sales, tech, accounts) to resolve issues.
  • Maintain communication loops with relevant stakeholders.

Merchant Relationship Executive (MRE)

The Merchant Relationship Executive is responsible for onboarding, supporting, and managing relationships with merchants/vendors. They act as the primary point of contact between the company and merchants, ensuring smooth operations, resolving issues, and helping merchants maximize their engagement with the platform.

1. Merchant Onboarding

  • Identify and onboard new merchants to the platform.
  • Assist merchants with documentation, account setup, and product/service listing.
  • Provide training on how to use the company’s tools or dashboard.

2. Issue Resolution

  • Handle operational or technical issues faced by merchants.
  • Coordinate with internal teams (support, tech, logistics) for resolution.
  • Ensure timely follow-up on open issues.

3. Sales & Growth Support

  • Encourage merchants to participate in campaigns, promotions, or new feature adoption.
  • Upsell value-added services (ads, premium listings, etc.).
  • Work towards increasing merchant sales and engagement.

User Relationship Executive (URE)

The User Relationship Executive is responsible for building and maintaining positive relationships with users/customers by providing support, guidance, and a high-quality experience. The goal is to increase user satisfaction, retention, and platform trust.

1. User Support & Engagement

  • Interact with users via phone, email, chat, or in-app support to resolve queries.
  • Provide step-by-step assistance on using the product or service.

2. Documentation & Reporting

  • Log interactions and updates in CRM or support tools.
  • Maintain accurate records of user issues, feedback, and behavior trends.
  • Provide regular reports on user satisfaction metrics and pain points.

3. Issue Resolution

  • Troubleshoot problems and ensure timely resolution.
  • Escalate complex issues to relevant departments (technical, billing, etc.).
  • Follow up proactively to ensure the issue is completely resolved.
